About Us

Sydney Harbour Runners has established themselves as Sydney's Bridge Runners with all our courses giving you great views of at least one of Sydney's iconcic bridges! We are a Community of runners, and we make our SHR run night relaxed and social, there is a pace group for most runners, and we welcome all new runners to come along. Weekly group runs are FREE and we meet on Tuesdays from 5:45pm at the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain at Darling Harbour!
  • Two distance options: short course around 6k-8k, long course around 11k-13k
  • 4:30, 5:00, 5:30, and 6:00min/km pace groups
  • Place to leave your bag
  • Post-Run meals and drink specials
  • Runs leave 6:15 from the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain
