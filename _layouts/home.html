Sydney Harbour Runners has established themselves as Sydney's Bridge Runners with all our courses giving you great views of at least one of Sydney's iconic bridges!

We are a Community of runners, and we make our SHR run nights relaxed and social, there is a pace group for most runners, and we welcome all new runners to come along.

Weekly group runs are FREE and we meet on Tuesdays from 5:45pm at the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain at Darling Harbour!
  • Two distance options: short course around 6k-8k, long course around 11k-13k
  • 4:30, 5:00, 5:30, and 6:00min/km pace groups
  • Place to leave your bag
  • Post-Run meals and drink specials
  • Runs leave 6:15 from the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain

Is SHR Running this week?

Yes! We will let you know in advance if we are not planning on running. You can also check our schedules page for information about future weeks.

How do I join?

Just rock up! There is no need to register and we are 100% free to join.
