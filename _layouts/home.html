Sydney Harbour Runners are a community of runners, and we make our SHR run nights relaxed and social. There is a pace group for most runners, and we welcome all new runners to come along.

Weekly group runs are FREE and we meet on Tuesdays from 6:00pm at the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain at Darling Harbour!
  • Two distance options: short course around 6k-8k, long course around 11k-13k
  • Pace groups from 4:30 to 6:15 min/km - our routes for each week are shown here
  • Place to leave your bag
  • Post-Run meals and drink specials
  • Runs leave 6:15pm from the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain

Is SHR Running this week?

Yes! We will let you know in advance if we are not planning on running.

You can also check our schedule page for information about future weeks.

Note that we will not be running on 4 Jun 2024 and 11 Jun 2024 due to Vivid, returning back to our regular schedule on 18 June 2024. You can also check our schedule page for information about future weeks.

When & Where do you meet?

Tuesdays at 6:00pm starting at the Cockle Bay Wharf Bird Fountain in Darling Harbour

How do I join?

Just rock up! There is no need to register and we are 100% free to join.

Any questions?

Please join the Sydney Harbour Runners Facebook group and make a post!
